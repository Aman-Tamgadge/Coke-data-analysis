#This GitHub repository contains a comprehensive sales analysis project focused on Coca-Cola beverage sales across various regions in the United States during the year 2021. The dataset used includes detailed sales information from key American retailers, covering multiple beverage brands such as Coca-Cola, Diet Coke, Sprite, Fanta, Powerade, and Dasani Water. Key metrics analyzed include units sold, price per unit, total sales, operating profit, and operating margin, allowing for a well-rounded assessment of product performance.

The project emphasizes understanding sales trends and profitability across different regions like the Northeast, South, Midwest, and West, focusing on cities including New York, Chicago, Houston, and Los Angeles. By cleaning and processing raw sales data, the project offers valuable insights into monthly sales patterns, regional market variations, and the impact of pricing strategies on sales and profit margins.

The repository features data preprocessing scripts, exploratory data analysis, and visualizations that highlight sales volumes, revenue trajectories, and profit margins by brand and location. Its purpose is to support sales teams, marketers, and data analysts with actionable insights to make informed decisions aimed at optimizing pricing, marketing campaigns, and distribution channels.

Primarily using Excel for data management, this project can be extended to incorporate advanced analytic tools in Python or other data visualization platforms to enhance the depth of insights. This repository serves as a practical example of applying data analytics and business intelligence techniques to drive better sales outcomes and improve operational efficiency in the consumer beverage sector.

Contributions are welcomed to expand the dataset, refine analytical methods, or add new visualization features, making it a versatile resource for professionals aiming to leverage data for strategic business growth.
